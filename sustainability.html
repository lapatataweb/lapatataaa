Our Sustainability Promise

We're committed to practices that cherish our planet, from the soil to your table.

90% Local Suppliers

We source the majority of our ingredients from local Virginia farms, minimizing our environmental impact and supporting our community.

Zero Food Waste

We meticulously plan our menus to minimize waste. Leftover ingredients are creatively repurposed, and food scraps are composted.

Compostable Packaging

We prioritize packaging that is compostable or recyclable, reducing our reliance on single-use plastics and minimizing our carbon footprint.

Plant-to-Plate Journey

1. Local Sourcing

Partnering with Virginia farms for fresh, seasonal ingredients.

2. Sustainable Farming

Prioritizing organic methods and soil health via our suppliers.

3. Mindful Transport

Short distances significantly reduce our carbon footprint.

4. Low-Waste Prep

Utilizing ingredients fully, from root to stem, minimizing waste.

5. Conscious Cooking

Crafting delicious, thoughtful plant-based meals with care.

6. Plating & Serving

Bringing the vibrant, sustainable dish directly to your table.

7. Closing the Loop

Composting all scraps and using eco-friendly packaging.
